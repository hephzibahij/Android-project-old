Android Docuseries

Introducing Being Human Is.

Welcome to Being Human Is, an animated docuseries. Meet musicians, athletes, filmmakers and others from around the world, fulfilling their passions and bringing their ideas to life. In ways big and small. With Android by their side.

Watch the series on YouTube

Episode 1: On Silent Ice

Anthony, a hockey champion, founded the Deaf Hockey Association to amplify the talent of deaf and hard of hearing players across Canada. And to get new players drafted to the CHL using Live Transcribe.

Meet Anthony

Episode 2: Na Cor

Mariluce paints colorful scenes inspired by her favela in Rio de Janeiro. She paints what she feels, reflecting the vibrancy of everyday life. Now, shes teaching students to do the same. And her Android phone keeps her students connected to their art, wherever it goes.

Meet Mariluce

Episode 3: Ramblin Man

Joshua is a traveling blind blues singer-songwriter who tours across the U.S. with his guitar and dog. Writing lyrics on the road, dictated to his Android with Talkback.

Meet Joshua

Episode 4: The Magic Makers

Raymond is fulfilling his dream of becoming a filmmaker, producing spectacular sci-fi films that create portals into new worlds. And bringing visual magic to every scene. Shot on his Android.

Meet Raymond

Episode 5: Chez Elyse

Elyse rediscovered her passion for professional cooking after adjusting to life with low vision. Now, she's a chef at a busy pub in England, and triple taps her phone to help during critical moments in the kitchen.

Meet Elyse
